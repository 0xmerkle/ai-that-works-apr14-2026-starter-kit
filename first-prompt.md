# Starter Prompts

Pick a skill, copy its prompt, paste it into Claude Code. Each one produces real results in 1-2 minutes.

---

## 1. Lead Generation
**Skill:** `apify-lead-generation` — Scrapes business directories and contact data

```
Find 50 coffee shops in Austin, TX with at least 4 stars on Google Maps.
Get their name, address, phone number, website, rating, and review count.
Export as CSV.
```

**What to expect:** A CSV file with 50 rows of real business data pulled from Google Maps.

---

## 2. Competitor Intelligence
**Skill:** `apify-competitor-intelligence` — Analyzes competitor reviews, pricing, and positioning

```
Compare these 3 Austin co-working spaces: Capital Factory, WeWork Austin,
and Industrious Austin. Scrape their Google Maps reviews and ratings.
Summarize what customers love and hate about each one.
```

**What to expect:** A side-by-side comparison with review sentiment, common praise, and common complaints.

---

## 3. Brand Monitoring
**Skill:** `apify-brand-reputation-monitoring` — Tracks reviews, ratings, and sentiment

```
Monitor what people are saying about Franklin Barbecue in Austin.
Scrape their latest Google Maps reviews. Run sentiment analysis and
tell me if the vibe is shifting — are people still as enthusiastic?
```

**What to expect:** A sentiment breakdown of recent reviews with trend analysis.

---

## 4. E-Commerce Research
**Skill:** `apify-ecommerce` — Pricing intelligence and product research

```
I'm launching a hot sauce brand in Austin. Search Amazon for the top 20
best-selling hot sauces. Get their prices, ratings, review counts, and
the most common complaints from 1-star reviews.
```

**What to expect:** A ranked list of hot sauce products with pricing data and complaint themes.

## 5. Content Analytics
**Skill:** `apify-content-analytics` — Measures engagement and content performance

```
Analyze the last 30 posts from @visitaustin on Instagram. Which content
types get the most engagement? What posting times work best?
```

**What to expect:** An engagement report showing top-performing content types and optimal posting windows.

## 6. Influencer Discovery
**Skill:** `apify-influencer-discovery` — Finds and vets influencers

```
Find 15 Austin-based food and lifestyle influencers on Instagram with
10k-100k followers. Get their engagement rate, average likes, and
posting frequency.
```

**What to expect:** A list of influencer profiles with engagement metrics, ready for outreach.

## 7. Market Research
**Skill:** `apify-market-research` — Analyzes market conditions and geographic opportunities

```
I want to open a yoga studio in Austin. How many yoga studios are there,
what are their ratings, and what neighborhoods are underserved?
Use Google Maps data.
```

**What to expect:** A market overview with competitor density by neighborhood and gap analysis.

## 8. Trend Analysis
**Skill:** `apify-trend-analysis` — Tracks emerging trends

```
What are the trending topics in Austin's tech scene? Check Google Trends
for "Austin AI", "Austin startups", and "Austin tech jobs" over the
last 90 days.
```

**What to expect:** Trend graphs and analysis showing which topics are gaining or losing interest.

## 9. Audience Analysis
**Skill:** `apify-audience-analysis` — Understands demographics and engagement patterns

```
Analyze the audience of @saboratx on Instagram. Who's engaging with
their content? What are the peak engagement times and what content
themes get the most interaction?
```

**What to expect:** A demographic and behavioral profile of who engages with this account.

## What's next?

After running a prompt, try customizing the skill. Ask Claude Code:

```
What skill did you just use? Show me the SKILL.md file.
```

Then tell it what to change — different filters, different data points, different city, different output format. Claude updates the skill and you run it again with different results.

Want to build a skill from scratch? See [`build-your-own-skill.md`](./build-your-own-skill.md).
